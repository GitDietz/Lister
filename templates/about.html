How this works

Manage lists - one or several different ones, each with different members

To Get started

Register and create a group in the process. Give the group a short name and purpose, then invite (Invite menu) people to join the group

You can create additional groups for different purposes (Group Menu)

Who can do what?

You as the creator of the group manages the group memberships

You can set members to be leaders / revoke the leader role or remove them from the group

As a Leader you can do?

Mark items as complete, delete them or edit them

As a member, you can...

Add Merchants (Merchant Menu item) to the merchant list for a group, Add items and edit your own items. See the items of others, but not delete/edit them
